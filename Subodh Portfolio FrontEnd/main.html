About Me

Hi, I'm Subodh R Bhat, a passionate web designer and developer. With a deep love for crafting beautiful and functional websites, I specialize in using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to bring ideas to life. I believe that design is not just about aesthetics but also about creating meaningful user experiences that drive engagement.

I started my journey in web development a few years ago, and since then, I have honed my skills by working on a variety of projects, ranging from personal websites to more complex web applications. My goal is to create responsive, clean, and efficient websites that not only look great but also perform seamlessly across all devices.

I am always eager to learn new technologies and explore innovative design trends. Currently, I am expanding my knowledge of modern frontend frameworks, UI/UX principles, and web accessibility standards to stay up-to-date with the ever-evolving web landscape.

When I’m not coding, you can find me exploring new anime, reading manga, or experimenting with new design concepts. I’m excited to continue growing as a developer and contributing to projects that push the boundaries of web design.

Feel free to reach out to me for collaboration, freelance work, or just a friendly chat about technology and design!
